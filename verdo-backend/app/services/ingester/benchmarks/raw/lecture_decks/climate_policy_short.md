# Climate Policy Overview (Short Deck)

## Slide 1: Emissions Baseline
- Track CO2, methane, and nitrous oxide inventories.
- Use sector-level baselines for power and transport.

## Slide 2: Policy Instruments
- Carbon taxes, cap-and-trade, and clean energy standards.
- Subsidies accelerate renewable adoption.

## Slide 3: Adaptation Planning
- Resilience investments for coastal infrastructure.
- Early warning systems reduce disaster impacts.
